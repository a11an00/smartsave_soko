Help Center

Need assistance? We're here to help.

How do i search for an item?

while in the home page input product such as dawaat or item category such as rice in the search menu . scroll downwards to see the search results displayed.

How to check specific item price from the supermarkets?

on the displayed search results click on the specific product and the comparison data will be tabulated

How do i add items to cart and compare prices for the whole cart across supermarkets?

click the add to cart button on the bottom of the specific item , and input all the items you want to shop in the same way . navigate to cart page view your items and modify the quantity,remove items and add as you please.At the bottom of the cart set your price criteria for split shopping and click the compare button
