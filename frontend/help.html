Help Center

Need assistance? We're here to help.

How do I place an order?

Browse products, add them to your cart and proceed to checkout.

How can I track my order?

Visit your profile and open My Orders to see your order status.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-Pesa, Debit Cards and Credit Cards.

Can I return a product?

Yes. Returns are accepted within seven days if the item is in its original condition.
